How to Play

Joining a Game

Upon loading the game page, you'll be prompted to join an existing game or create a new one. If you're starting fresh, create a game and share the unique game code with your friends to play together. If you've been invited to a game, simply enter the provided code to join.

Understanding the Board

The game board consists of a 9x9 grid divided into smaller 3x3 subgrids. Your goal is to fill in each cell of the grid with a number from 1 to 9. However, each number can only appear once in each row, column, and subgrid.

Interacting with the Board

To select a cell on the board, simply click on it. Once selected, you can input a number by typing it on your keyboard. If you make a mistake, you can erase the number by pressing the delete or backspace key.

Pencil Mode

If you're unsure about a number but want to keep it in mind, you can enable pencil mode by clicking the pencil icon. In this mode, you can add small numbers (pencil marks) to a cell to indicate potential options. Toggle pencil mode on and off as needed.

Playing with Friends

This game supports multiplayer functionality, allowing you to play with friends in real-time. You can see each other's moves and progress on the board. Work together to solve the puzzle efficiently!

Checking for Mistakes

If you're unsure whether your board is correct, you can check for mistakes by clicking the "Check" button. Incorrect numbers will be highlighted in red, helping you identify and correct errors.

Communicating with Players

Use the chat feature to communicate with other players in the game. Discuss strategies, share hints, or simply chat while playing.

Winning the Game

The game is won when every cell on the board is filled correctly according to Sudoku rules. Once you've completed the puzzle, celebrate your victory with your fellow players!

Enjoying the Experience

Sudoku is a game of logic and strategy, offering a challenging yet rewarding experience. Take your time, think ahead, and enjoy the thrill of solving puzzles together with friends.
